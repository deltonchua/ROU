Business Administration
BScBA

ROU Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BScBA) is a 3 to 4 years American degree program that will have 15 modules & 45 credits in Freshman; 15 modules & 45 credits in Sophomore; 15 modules & 45 credits in Junior and 16 modules & 48 credits in Senior year. This is an on-line program that is suitable for people who are busy and always on the move but hungry for formal education.

Entry Requirements:

You should have completed your high school studies or age above 16 years old with good spoken and written English (IELTS 5.0 or equivalent).
