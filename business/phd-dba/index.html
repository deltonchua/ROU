Business Administration
PhD / DBA

ROU Doctor of Philosophy / Doctor in Business Administration (PhD/DBA) is a Doctoral program that is designed for people who have the right attitude: “Yes I can”, “Nothing is impossible”, “Nothing is an emergency”, “Cash is King”, “Time is money”, “Plan and do now - positive and always motivated personality”, “Never give up”, “Very passionate in finding solution” and many more. It has 10% (31 modules) refresher program from the MScBA and the balance of 90% (7 modules) is the big data research and business need elements toward the final dissertation. This is a no-nonsense ROU PhD/DBA that is equivalent to building your own business from zero to earn a billionaire seat on the Forbes World's Billionaires list.

The focus remains on original, enhancing your career / business yielding capabilities and operational effectiveness and efficiencies, you also will learn SOP implementation on how to embrace change throughout your career / business life journey.

Entry Requirements:

You should have a Master’s Degree in any business-related subject, or a combination of relevant professional experience and formal qualifications, with minimum Grade Point Average (GPA) of 3.0 or equivalent. You should also have a good written and spoken English (IELTS 6.0 or equivalent).

Applicant must submit two recommendation letters supporting the applicant’s ability to succeed in a doctoral program and an easy of your Dissertation objective(s) in no less than 1,000 words.
