About ROU

Royal Oxford University (ROU) is a university that will have the 5 quality elements below:

  1. ROU chancellors:
    1. At the United Kingdom campus will be the Honorable King or Queen of the UK
    2. At the United States Of America campus will be the Honorable President of the USA
    3. At Canada Campus will be the Honorable Prime Minister of the Canada
    4. At Myanmar Campus will be the Honorable President or the Head of State of Myanmar
  2. Alumni:
    1. King & Queen, Trillionaires & Billionaires, Country Leaders (Chairman, President & Prime Minister) are automatically invited as ROU Lifetime Diamond members and Honorable PhD/DBA of ROU graduates.
    2. All Multi-Millionaires are automatically invited as ROU Lifetime Gold members and Honorable MScBABA of ROU graduates.
    3. ll students who graduated from ROU are Lifetime Gold member for MScBABA, Lifetime Diamond member for PhD/DBA and others as Ordinary member.
  3. Research:

    ROU will contribute 5% of its annual net profit and all Alumni membership fees for ROU research projects.

    1. Medical cure: Dengue, Leukemia
    2. Cloning of Mammoth, Rafflesia and extinct plants & animals
    3. DNA on strengthening immunity and controlling appetite
    4. Artificial Intelligent on Neuro and peta technology
    5. Increase safe food production – plants & animals, water, air etc.
    6. Recycle waste, reduce pollution & waste and reuse waste
    7. Logistic & Warehousing and E-store & E-payment
    8. Big data analysis by quantum computing
    9. Portable solar energy
    10. High rise, high speed and self-sustainable buildings
    11. Induced pluripotent and mesenchymal stem cells (iPSC & MSC) towards kidney printing
    12. Nuclear energy
    13. Starlink WiFi
    14. Neuralink technology
    15. Blockchain, crypto money, and ethereum swarm technology
    16. "ICU": "I See You" AI App
  4. Global Cares: Plant trees and support hard core poor orphans to get job.
  5. Embarking on a 11 star education:
    1. * All Lecturers Are PhD holders from UK/USA/Canada
    2. * Flexible Entry Requirements
    3. * Jobs Ready-Link Upon Graduation
    4. * Attend Only 4 Times in a Year
    5. * No Exam & 100% Assignment-Based
    6. * Flexible Enrolment (PT/FT/On Line)
    7. * Millionaires MScBABA & Billionaires PhD/DBA
    8. * World Class Top 7 Universities Standard
    9. * Double Certification From UK/USA/Canada
    10. * All Will Graduate With a E-Business portal
    11. * World No. 1 & First MScBABA & PhD/DBA:
      1. * With 31 Modules
      2. * Most Comprehensive & Practical

We will teach & coach all ROU students to have the ability and passion to work wisely, creatively, effectively, and efficiently to serve the community, nation and the world for the betterment of human kind at present & future.
