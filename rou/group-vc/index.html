Prof. Dr. Christina Smith
Group Vice-Chancellor, Royal Oxford University

We welcome you to the website of the Royal Oxford University (ROU), the experience, business and research university for studying full time, part-time and distance learning/online. This University is always open for every citizen staying anywhere in the world. This university provides an education of international standards at your doorstep with an affordable cost including the course materials with a business e-store portal for each student.

As the Group Vice-Chancellor of ROU, my priority will be to strengthen the quality and practical value of education, especially for those deprived and in rural areas of each country. We have a highly qualified and experienced faculty from UK, USA, Germany, Canada, Ireland and selected professors from other countries. You can visit the nearest campuses/offices to obtain or download the ROU prospectus and admission details of your desired program online.

Today, modern Information and Communication Technology (ICT) is playing significant role in providing facilities for the delivery of quality education. ROU is making new strides in employing the most modern communication technology for effective delivery of its education. The rapid advancement in ICT has helped a great deal in eliminating the physical distance. With the latest innovations in computer and communication technology, the world has become a global village where information is readily available and accessible to everyone. ROU has taken full advantage of the modern ICT to facilitate and support its students all over the world.

Our key focus is our ongoing research programs stated below and global activities (plant trees and help the poor & under privileged children’s education).

  1. Medical cure: Dengue, Leukemia and life-threatening diseases to mankind
  2. Cloning of Mammoth and extinct animals
  3. DNA on strengthening immunity and controlling appetite
  4. Artificial Intelligence, Neuro and peta technology
  5. Increase safe food production – plants & animals, water, air etc.
  6. Recycle waste, reduce pollution & waste and reuse waste
  7. Logistic & Warehousing and E-store & E-payment (Fiat & Crypto Money)
  8. Big data analysis programming
  9. Portable solar energy application in nano size
  10. High rise, high speed and self-sustainable buildings
  11. Hologram Technology for education life lecturing
  12. Software engineering for finance & banking
  13. ICT: Information and Communication Technology
  14. Micropropagation / plant tissue culture of Rafflesia and extinct plants
  15. Mesenchymel Stem Cells (MSC) application research (PRP & slowing down aging process)
  16. Induced Pluripotent Stem Cell (IPSC) application research
  17. Blockchain and Crypto Technology
  18. Artificial Intelligent (AI) related research projects (ICU, Jobs, Assets, Edu…)
  19. Quantum Technology research
  20. Nano Nuclear Power Technology
  21. Robotic Chef application

We wish all our learners/students a very bright future. I do believe that after getting educated at ROU, you will become an asset for your country and the world that you will be able to play an important role in accelerating the rate of economic & social development of your country and internationally. Let your dreams come true.
